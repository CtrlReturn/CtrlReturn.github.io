LIVE CODE AND LET CODE LIVE

Our motivation stems from the belief that strengthening coding skills in creative self-expression enhances its appeal. By integrating Coding into music creation, we tap into the universal language of music using different symbols.

This initiative aims to stimulate engagement with coding and programming. Recognizing the significant role music plays in shaping identities, we believe our project will bring individuals into contact with Computational Arts and its vibrant community, fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration.
